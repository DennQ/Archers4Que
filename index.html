What is Archer's Network?

Archers Network is the PREMIER online television network organization of De La Salle University, made for Lasallians, by Lasallians. We serve as a broadcast medium to the Lasallian community through social media.

Through the five channels: Advertisements, Entertainment, Events, News & Current Affairs, and Sports & Culture, we focus on producing relevant, informative, and entertaining content.
